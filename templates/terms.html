TenderAI use policy

Terms and Conditions

These terms explain how TenderAI should be used when preparing tender documents.

TenderAI is an assistant

TenderAI helps users find public opportunities, organize company documents, extract requirements, and prepare draft bid documents. It does not replace professional judgment, legal review, procurement review, or final bidder responsibility.

Review before submission

Generated forms, checklists, letters, schedules, and bid packs may contain mistakes or missing information. Users must review every document against the original solicitation document and ZPPA instructions before submitting a bid.

Public data only

TenderAI should only scrape or import public tender information. It must not bypass logins, payments, authentication, private groups, or protected pages.

User responsibility

Each user is responsible for the accuracy of company details, certificates, prices, signatures, attachments, and final bid submission. TenderAI is provided to make the process easier and faster, not to guarantee bid compliance or award.
