How to use TenderAI

TenderAI workflow guide

TenderAI helps you find public ZPPA tenders, check which company is ready to bid, manage certificates, analyze solicitation documents, and generate bid packs for human review.

1

Scrape ZPPA tenders

Open the Dashboard or Tenders page and click Scrape ZPPA. TenderAI imports current public ZPPA tender listings and puts the latest tenders first.

Open tenders
2

Filter and choose a tender

Use the tender filters to search by procuring entity, period, title, tender number, or ZPPA resource ID. Open the tender that looks relevant.

View closing tenders
3

Upload company documents

Add PACRA, TPIN, ZRA Tax Clearance, NAPSA, Workers Compensation, ZPPA, NCC, ERB/EIZ, bank letters, company profiles, and past contracts.

Upload document
4

Check company readiness

Open Companies to see readiness scores, missing fields, expired documents, and document gaps before deciding which company should bid.

Open companies
5

Match companies to tender

On the tender page, click Should we bid?. TenderAI compares the tender requirements against each company and recommends the best option.

Choose tender
6

Upload solicitation document

On a tender page, upload the PDF, DOCX, XML, or text solicitation file. TenderAI extracts requirements, dates, ITB signals, required forms, and document evidence.

Open tender page
7

Ask TenderAI questions

Use the quick questions to ask what documents are required, what can disqualify you, important dates, and required forms.

Use TenderAI chat
8

Create and download bid pack

From the tender detail page, click Generate bid documents, choose the company, review readiness, then download individual PDFs or a combined pack.

Open bid packs

Daily routine

  1. Start TenderAI from the desktop shortcut.
  2. Click Scrape ZPPA.
  3. Review alerts for closing tenders and expiring documents.
  4. Open promising tenders and check the XML evidence checklist.
  5. Fix missing documents before generating a bid pack.

Important notes

  • TenderAI only scrapes public ZPPA tender pages.
  • It does not bypass logins or protected pages.
  • Rule-based AI/XML analysis should be checked against the original solicitation document.
  • Always confirm deadlines, bid security, site visits, and mandatory forms before submission.
  • TenderAI helps compile documents, but the bidder remains responsible for final review and submission.
